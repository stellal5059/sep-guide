What is Robotics?


Robotics is the branch of computer science that deals with design, buliding, action and applicance of roberts.

Tips for being successful when learning about Robotics

  • If you have a question ask 3 before asking the teacher for help
  • If you don't know something a good tool to use would be Google
    ~Google is your best friend~
  • Don't fool around in class or you will get left behind
  • Never be shy to share your answer with the class, you are never wrong unless you make an educated guess
