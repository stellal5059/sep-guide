Scratch



What is Scratch and has it helped us better become engineers?


Scratch is a programming langauge as well as a online community while people of all ages can share stories, games, etc... with people all over the world. Working on Scratch has taught us how to think creatively, work with other members and come up with better solutions for a problem.



Tips for being successful when learning about Scratch


  • When working on Scratch pay very close attention to small details
    The smallest problem could destroy your whole project
  • (3 before me!) Ask 3 of your classmates for help before asking your teacheer
  • Look at other people's projects for inspiration
