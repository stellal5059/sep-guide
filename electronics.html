Electronics

What are electronics?

Electronics is a hands on unit where we use wires and codes to make simple visual projects. For example, electronics can be used to make a simple circuit that will have little lights glow in certain patterns.

Tips to be successful with electronics

  • Becareful, wires can be dangerous
  • Always pay attention to how the wires and lights are plugged in, that can be the source of many problems
  • Pay attention to code syntax, it can get confusing
